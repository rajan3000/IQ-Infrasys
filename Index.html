About Us

IQ Infrasys is a trusted and fast-growing system integrator delivering advanced technology solutions across India. We specialize in Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), Toll Management Systems, Smart Parking Solutions, Data Centres, Audio-Visual Systems, and Integrated Security Solutions. With a strong foundation in engineering excellence and project execution, we design, integrate, and maintain mission-critical infrastructure that supports smart cities, highways, urban mobility, commercial complexes, and government projects. Our solutions are built to be scalable, reliable, and future-ready, ensuring long-term performance and value. At IQ Infrasys, innovation drives everything we do. We leverage the latest technologies, industry best practices, and trusted global partners to deliver solutions that enhance operational efficiency, safety, and data intelligence. From planning and design to installation, commissioning, and ongoing support, we offer end-to-end system integration services. Our experienced team of engineers and professionals is committed to quality, compliance, and timely delivery.
